About Me

I am a multifaceted professional with a strong background in computer science, IT consultancy, and education. With a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Mathematics from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture & Technology and a Diploma in Technical Teacher Education (Advanced) from Kenya Technical Trainers College, my career has been dedicated to merging the realms of technology and education.

In the IT sector, I have extensive experience as a software developer and IT consultant, collaborating with companies like Kwality Consulting Solutions to design and implement client-specific software solutions. My expertise includes managing cloud server deployments, enhancing user engagement through innovative features, and conducting comprehensive business analyses to drive decision-making.

As a teacher, I have over six years of experience inspiring and motivating students from diverse backgrounds. My teaching philosophy emphasizes hands-on, project-based learning, and I strive to create an inclusive and engaging classroom environment. This approach fosters creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration, empowering the next generation of computer scientists. In my free time, I enjoy staying updated with the latest trends in technology and engaging in community service projects. I am passionate about mentoring young aspiring computer scientists and continuously seek to bridge the gap between technology and education to make meaningful contributions to society.
